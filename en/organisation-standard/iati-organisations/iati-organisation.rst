Example Usage
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Example of ``iati-organisation`` with ``last updated-datetime`` of on 6th May 2014. 
Language set as *en* (English) and a ``default-currency`` to *EU* (Euros).  
The optional ``version`` of *1.04* is also stated:
